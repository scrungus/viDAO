StreamDAO is a video streaming platform built around a single idea: every pound subscribers pay goes directly to creators. There is no advertising, no platform cut beyond a 5% protocol fee, and no opaque algorithm deciding who gets paid. Creators earn proportionally to how much verified time viewers spend watching their content.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe">
    Enter your email and card details on the sign-up page. Fiat is converted to USDC automatically — you never need to touch crypto.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your device">
    Your browser will prompt you for a fingerprint, Face ID, or PIN once. This registers your device and ensures your watch time counts toward creator payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch and support creators">
    Stream any content on the platform. As you watch, your time is verified and attributed to creators — they earn from the shared pool weekly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  StreamDAO currently runs on Arbitrum Sepolia testnet. Real USDC subscriptions and mainnet launch are coming soon.
</Note>
