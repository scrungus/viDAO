Getting started on viDAO takes less than five minutes. You'll enter your email, pay by card, and tap through one security prompt from your browser. After that, you're watching — and every minute you spend is automatically counted toward creator earnings.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For viewers">
    ## Sign up and start watching

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit the viDAO homepage">
        Go to the viDAO homepage. You'll see a grid of videos from creators on the platform. You can browse thumbnails without an account — no login required to look around.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Click any video to be prompted to subscribe">
        When you click a video to watch, you'll be asked to create an account. Select **Subscribe** to begin. You don't need to choose a plan — there's one flat monthly subscription that gives you full access to everything.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Enter your email and card details">
        Enter your email address and payment details on the sign-up screen. viDAO accepts standard card payments — Visa, Mastercard, and others. You do not need a cryptocurrency wallet, a crypto exchange account, or any knowledge of blockchain to continue.

        Your card payment is converted to USDC automatically in the background. This conversion is handled securely and is invisible to you — you pay in your local currency and viDAO takes care of the rest.

        <Info>
          USDC is a digital currency that's always worth one US dollar. viDAO uses it to pay creators across borders instantly. You'll never need to interact with it directly.
        </Info>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Register your device with a single biometric prompt">
        After your payment is confirmed, your browser will show a prompt asking for your fingerprint, Face ID, or device PIN. Tap or scan to confirm.

        This is a one-time step that ties your account to your physical device. It uses a security standard built into your phone or computer — the same technology that protects banking apps and password managers. Your device generates a security key inside a protected chip, and that key is used to verify your watch sessions are genuine.

        <Note>
          This prompt comes from your browser or operating system, not viDAO. viDAO never sees your fingerprint or biometric data — only a confirmation from your device that you approved the request.
        </Note>

        You won't be prompted again on this device. If you sign in on a new device later, you'll go through the same quick step once on that device too.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Watch content — your time is counted automatically">
        You'll be taken straight to the video you clicked. Press play and watch normally. There's nothing to configure, no timers to start, and no check-ins required.

        While you watch, your device quietly confirms your session every 30 seconds in the background. These confirmations accumulate as verified watch hours and are used at the end of each week to determine how creator payouts are split.

        <Tip>
          Your watch time counts toward creator earnings for up to 50 hours per month. If you watch more than that — great — but the pool weight stops accumulating at 50 hours. This cap keeps things fair for everyone.
        </Tip>
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ## Watching on additional devices

    You can use viDAO on multiple devices, but each one needs to go through the one-time device registration step. Sign in with your account on the new device and you'll be prompted to register it before watch time starts counting.

    <Warning>
      Only devices with built-in hardware security (iPhones from 2013+, Android phones from 2018+, Windows 10+ PCs with TPM 2.0) can accumulate watch time that counts toward creator earnings. Older devices can still watch all content — their time just won't contribute to the pool split.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="For creators">
    ## Join as a creator

    <Note>
      Creator accounts are invite-only during viDAO's current launch phase. This lets the team review content and ensure the platform is working well before opening to everyone. If you'd like to join, apply through the creator waitlist and you'll be contacted when a spot opens.
    </Note>

    Once you receive your invite, here's how to get set up:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Accept your invite and connect your wallet">
        Click the link in your invite email. You'll be asked to connect a crypto wallet — this is where your USDC earnings will be sent each week. If you don't have a wallet, the onboarding flow will help you create one. Unlike viewers, creators need a wallet address to receive payouts.

        <Info>
          viDAO pays creators in USDC on Arbitrum — a fast, low-cost network. You'll need a wallet that supports Arbitrum, such as MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet. Your invite email includes setup instructions if this is new to you.
        </Info>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Upload your first video via the creator dashboard">
        Once your account is active, go to your **Creator Dashboard** and click **Upload video**. Add your video file, a title, a description, and a thumbnail. Videos are processed and made available to subscribers once they pass a brief manual review.

        Keep your thumbnail at a 16:9 aspect ratio for the best appearance in the content grid.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Track your earnings and watch stats">
        Your dashboard shows your total earnings for the current period, how many verified watch hours your content has received, and a breakdown by individual video. Payouts run weekly — you'll see your USDC balance update in your connected wallet after each distribution.

        <Tip>
          Earnings are proportional to verified watch hours across the whole platform. Growing your share means attracting genuine viewers who watch your content fully — not just clicks or impressions.
        </Tip>
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ## How creator payouts work

    Your weekly earnings are calculated as:

    ```
    your verified watch hours ÷ total platform watch hours × pool size
    ```

    The pool is funded by subscriber fees collected that month (minus the 5% protocol fee). Payouts are distributed automatically each week — you don't need to request them or submit any paperwork.

    For a detailed breakdown of how the pool is calculated, see [How it works](/how-it-works).
