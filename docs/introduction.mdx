StreamDAO is a video streaming platform with one guiding principle: the money viewers pay should go to the people who make the content. Every pound from your monthly subscription — minus a 5% protocol fee — flows directly into a shared pool that creators draw from at the end of each week. There is no advertising, no opaque algorithm, and no platform taking a 30–45% cut before creators see a penny.

## What makes StreamDAO different

Most streaming platforms either sell advertising against your attention or take a substantial slice of revenue before passing anything to creators. StreamDAO removes both of those. The subscription pool belongs to creators from the moment subscriptions are paid — the platform takes only a small protocol fee to keep the lights on.

## How payouts work (the short version)

Subscribers pay a flat monthly fee. That money converts to USDC — a digital currency pegged to the dollar — and sits in a shared pool. At the end of each week, the pool is divided among creators in proportion to the verified watch hours their content received. A creator whose videos account for 10% of all verified watch time that week receives 10% of the pool.

<Info>
  USDC is a stablecoin: its value doesn't fluctuate the way that Bitcoin or other cryptocurrencies do. One USDC is always worth one US dollar. StreamDAO uses it for payouts because it settles instantly and moves across borders without a bank in the middle.
</Info>

## No crypto knowledge needed

StreamDAO uses blockchain technology under the hood, but you will never interact with it directly. There are no wallet addresses to copy, no gas fees to pay, and no tokens to manage. If you've used any streaming service before, the experience is the same — you pay by card and you watch.

<Tip>
  When you sign up, your browser will ask for a fingerprint, Face ID, or PIN once. That's a security step that protects your account and ensures your watch time is counted fairly. It takes a second and you won't be asked again on the same device.
</Tip>

## Who StreamDAO is for

StreamDAO is for viewers who want to support creators directly without wading through ads, and for creators who want a predictable, fair income tied to genuine audience engagement rather than ad revenue or platform favour.

The platform is currently in its early testing phase on Arbitrum Sepolia — a testnet environment where everything works as it will at launch, but real money isn't yet involved. Creator sign-ups are invite-only for now while the platform is being refined.
