Your creator dashboard is the central place to track how your content is performing and how much you've earned. Navigate to the **Dashboard** section after signing in — everything shown there is specific to your account: your videos, your watch hours, your payouts.

## Dashboard overview

When you open the dashboard, you'll see three main areas: a row of summary cards at the top, a video list below, and an upload button in the top-right corner.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Total earnings" icon="dollar-sign">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Watch hours this period" icon="clock">
    Verified watch hours accumulated across all your videos since the last monthly pool reset.

## Earnings card

The earnings figure shows your total USDC received for the current payout period. This number updates after each weekly payout run — it won't change in real time as viewers watch your content, only after the weekly aggregation completes and the transfer is processed.

<Info>
  "This period" refers to the current monthly cycle. Since the pool resets monthly, earnings shown here represent what you've received since the start of the current calendar month.
</Info>

## Watch hours cards

The two watch hours cards give you a quick comparison between your current period and the one before it. Use this to gauge whether your content is growing or shrinking in reach.

**Watch hours this period** counts only verified hours — viewers on attested devices with active subscriptions, up to their 50-hour monthly cap. Raw play counts are not shown because unverified plays don't affect your earnings.

**Watch hours last period** shows the equivalent verified total from the previous monthly cycle, giving you a direct baseline for comparison.

<Tip>
  If your watch hours this period are tracking below last period midway through the month, consider uploading new content. More videos give more surface area for viewers to find you.
</Tip>

## Video list

Below the summary cards, you'll see a list of every video you've uploaded. Each row shows:

- **Video title**
- **Watch hours** for that video in the current period
- **Earnings** attributed to that video

This breakdown lets you see which videos are driving most of your watch time. A video with consistently high hours across multiple periods is worth paying attention to — it's the kind of content your audience responds to.

<Accordion title="Why don't watch hours add up exactly to my total?">
  Watch hours in the video list reflect verified hours per video. There can be small rounding differences in how hours are displayed at the video level versus the account total, but your payout is always calculated from the authoritative server-side total, not the displayed per-video figures.
</Accordion>

## When stats update

| Metric | Update frequency |
|---|---|
| Watch hours (this period) | Accumulate in near real time as viewers watch |
| Watch hours (last period) | Fixed — reflects the final total from the closed period |
| Earnings | Updated after each weekly payout run |
| Per-video watch hours | Accumulate in near real time |

<Note>
  There can be a short delay of a few minutes between a verified viewing session and the watch hours appearing on your dashboard. This is normal — sessions are validated server-side before being credited.
</Note>

## Uploading from the dashboard

The **Upload** button is in the top-right corner of the dashboard. Clicking it opens the upload form where you can add a new video to your catalogue. See the [uploading guide](/creators/uploading) for full instructions on file requirements and the upload process.
