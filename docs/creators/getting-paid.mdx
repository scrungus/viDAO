Every time a subscriber watches your content, they're contributing to a shared pool that gets distributed to you and every other creator on the platform. There's no advertising, no platform taking a 30–45% cut — almost the entire subscription fee flows directly to creators, split by how much verified watch time your content earns.

## Where the money comes from

StreamDAO charges subscribers a flat monthly fee. After a small ~5% protocol fee to keep the network running, the remaining balance goes into a shared USDC pool. Every subscription renewal that month adds to that pool.

At the end of each payout period, that pool is divided among all creators proportionally — the more of the platform's total verified watch time your content earned, the larger your share.

<Info>
  USDC is a stable digital currency pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. You don't need to understand crypto to receive it — it lands in your connected earnings wallet automatically.
</Info>

## How your share is calculated

Your payout is calculated using a simple formula:

> **Your earnings = (your verified watch hours ÷ total platform watch hours) × pool balance**

## What counts as verified watch time

Not every play counts. Watch hours only accumulate when all of the following are true for the viewer:

- They have an **active subscription**
- They're watching on a **hardware-attested device** (modern iPhone, Android, Windows PC, or Mac — essentially any device from the last 5–8 years)
- They haven't yet hit the **50-hour monthly cap** per viewer

The hardware attestation requirement is how StreamDAO prevents fake views from inflating creator payouts. Each verified session is cryptographically signed by the viewer's physical device — VMs, bots, and headless browsers can't produce these signatures.

<Note>
  Viewers can watch your content on any device. The attestation requirement only affects whether their watch time counts toward your earnings — it doesn't gate access to your videos.
</Note>

## When you get paid

Payouts run on a **weekly schedule**. Each week, the platform aggregates all verified watch hours, calculates each creator's proportional share of that week's accumulated pool, and transfers USDC directly to your connected earnings wallet. No action required on your part.

<Accordion title="When does the pool reset?">
  The pool resets monthly, aligned with the subscription billing cycle. Each calendar month starts fresh — watch hours from previous months don't carry over into the new calculation. This means your earnings in any given week reflect your share of the hours accumulated since the last monthly reset.
</Accordion>

## Payout timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Viewers watch your content">
    Every 30 seconds of verified viewing on an attested device adds to your accumulated watch hours for the period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Weekly aggregation runs">
    At the end of each week, the platform tallies verified watch hours across all creators and calculates proportional shares of the pool.
  </Step>
  <Step title="USDC transferred to your wallet">
    Your share is sent automatically to your connected earnings wallet. No claim button, no manual step — it arrives directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pool resets monthly">
    At the start of each calendar month, the pool balance resets and the cycle begins again with the new month's subscription revenue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Growing your earnings

<Tip>
  Longer videos don't automatically earn more — watch time only counts while a viewer is actively watching. Content that keeps people engaged to the end earns proportionally more than content they abandon after a minute.
</Tip>

The most reliable way to grow your earnings is to grow your verified watch hours. That means uploading consistently, making content viewers finish, and building an audience of real, subscribed viewers. Because the pool is shared across all creators, your earnings grow both when your absolute watch hours increase and when your share of platform-wide hours grows.
