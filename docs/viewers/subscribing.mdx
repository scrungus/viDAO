viDAO gives you unlimited access to every video on the platform for a flat monthly fee. Behind the scenes, your subscription funds a shared pool that gets paid out directly to the creators you watch — with no platform taking a 30–45% cut. You pay with a regular card; everything else happens automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Click **Subscribe** on the homepage. You'll be taken to the sign-up flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and card details">
    Provide your email address and card details. This is a standard card checkout — the same experience you'd expect from any streaming service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your device">
    Your browser will prompt you once to confirm your identity using Face ID, a fingerprint, or your device PIN. This takes about one second and only happens during sign-up. It's how viDAO verifies that your watch time is genuine and ensures it counts toward creator payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start watching">
    That's it. You'll land on the homepage with full access to all content. Your subscription renews automatically each month.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's included

A viDAO subscription gives you:

- **Unlimited viewing** — access to all content on the platform, with no limits on how much you can watch
- **Verified watch time** — the time you spend watching counts toward creator payouts, so creators earn based on real engagement rather than ad clicks
- **No ads** — viDAO has no advertising

## How your payment reaches creators

When you subscribe, your payment is converted into a stablecoin (USDC) and added to a shared monthly pool. You never see this happening — there's no crypto wallet to manage, no gas fees, and no token names to understand. At the end of each period, the pool is distributed to creators in proportion to how many verified watch hours they received.

<Info>
  The conversion from your card payment to USDC is handled entirely server-side. You interact only with a standard card form.
</Info>

## Subscription renewal and cancellation

Your subscription renews automatically every month on the same date you first subscribed. If you cancel, your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

<Note>
  Watch time you accumulate during an active subscription counts toward creator payouts in real time. If your subscription lapses, watch time stops accumulating — but you can resubscribe at any time to resume.
</Note>
